Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our events, resources, and the universe.

How do I join a stargazing event?

You can join by signing up on our events page and selecting a gathering near you or online.

Are the events suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our events welcome all experience levels, and experts are always available to guide you.

What equipment do I need?

Some events provide telescopes, but you can also bring your own binoculars or telescope for a better view.

Can I ask astronomers questions during live sessions?

Yes, our sessions are interactive, and you’re encouraged to ask questions and participate.

Is there a membership or fee?

Many resources and events are free; some workshops may have a small fee to support our expert hosts.

How is my privacy protected?

Your information is kept private and secure in accordance with our privacy policy.
